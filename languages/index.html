I was not able to find much material for Western Armenian which includes some audio for beginning learners. There is a very short Western Armenian course by Pimsleur (ten 30-minute lessons), but luckily there also exist some freely accessible resources:

  • The public domain book A Textbook of Modern Western Armenian by Kevork B. Bardakjian and Robert W. Thomson, published by Caravan Books in 1977.
  • Free audio recordings for A Textbook of Modern Western Armenian, courtesy of the Language Resource Center of the University of Michigan. There is a total of 9h19m of MP3 audio (about 3h14m when the silent portions are deleted).
  • I am writing solutions to the translation exercises from A Textbook of Modern Western Armenian as I read the book (work in progress ðŸš§).
